COVID-19 Information for Research Community

As the COVID-19 pandemic presents unprecedented challenges to all facets of society, including research institutions like ours, we are sharing the below guidance to help you manage your research projects, laboratories and scholarly activities. You should know that the Division for Research stands ready to assist you in any way we can, whether with pre- or post-award management, or with further contingency planning, should you need assistance or support. 

As government officials continue to work through this dynamic situation, the University at Albany, in collaboration with the State University of New York, the Research Foundation for SUNY (RF-SUNY) and campuses system-wide, is working to develop guidance in support of the continuing operation of our institution’s research activities.

Our COVID-19 research guidance and resources webpage will provide you access to important resources to reference during the COVID-19 outbreak. Here you will find guidance from the University at Albany, SUNY, RF-SUNY (including pre- and post-award guidance), federal sponsors and agencies, resources and tools (e.g., laboratory shut down checklist), as well as points of contact in the Division for Research and the Colleges and Schools.

Below is a list of important links for our University at Albany research community during the COVID-19 outbreak. Should you have any questions regarding a proposal or an award, please contact your Research/Grants Administrator. For general questions regarding sponsored programs, please email spa@albany.edu. For any questions regarding regulatory and research compliance, research involving human subjects (IRB), or use of animals in research or teaching (IACUC, LAR), please contact the Office of Regulatory and Research Compliance at orrc@albany.edu.

Follow us @ResearchUAlbany on Twitter for latest updates.
